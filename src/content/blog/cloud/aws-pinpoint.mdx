- Scalable 2-way Marketing communication service.
- Supports Email, SMS, push, voice, in-app messaging.
- Ability to segment & personalize messages with the right content to customers.
- Possiblity to recieve replies
- Scale to billions of message/day
- Usecase: Run campaign by sending marketing bulk, transactional SMS messages.

- Compairison with AWS SNS & SES:
- In SNS & SES, you manage each message audience content and delivery schedule.
- In AWS Pinpoint you create message temlate segments delivery schedule, highly targetter segments & full campaigns.
