The intent of this service by AWS , is for people who have littlecloud experience
and dont want to learn how the service works internally.

- It provides <mark>Virtual Servers, Storage Databases & Networking</mark>
- Low & Predictable Pricing
- Simpler alternative to using EC2, RDS, ELB, EBS, Route53
- Great for people with litle cloud experience.
- Can setup notification & monitoring of your LightSail Resources
  <br />

```markdown
- Usecase:
  - Simple WebApp : has templets for LAMP, NGNIX, MEAN, Node.JS
  - Website : Wordpress, Magneto, Plesk, Joomla templates available.
  - Dev/Test enviornment.
```

<br />- Has high Availablity but no auto-scaling & Limited-AWS integration.
