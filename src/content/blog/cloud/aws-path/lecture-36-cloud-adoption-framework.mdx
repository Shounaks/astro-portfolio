- Helps you build and execute a comprehensive plan for your digital transformation through innovate use of AWS.
- Created by AWS Professionals by taking advantage of AWS best practices & lessions learnt from 1000s of customers.
- It identifies specific organizational capablities that underpin successful cloud transformation.
- AWS CAF Groups its capablities into & prespective.

<br />
<Section link="design-principles-b" title="B: Business Prespective" /> 
Helps ensure that your cloud investments acclerate your digital transformation ambition & business outcome.

<br />
<Section link="design-principles-p" title="P: People Prespective" /> 
Serves as a bridge between technology & business; Acclerating the cloud journey to help organise more rapidly evolve to a culture of continious,
growth, learning, and where changes become business-as-normal with focus-on-culture, learning, where change become leadership & workload.

<br />
<Section link="design-principles-g" title="G: Governance Prespective" /> 
Help you orchestrate your cloud initiatives while maximizing organisational benifits and minimizing transformation related risks.

<br />
<Section link="design-principles-pl" title="P: Platform Prespective" /> 
Helps you build enterprise grade, scalable, hybrid cloud platform modernize existing workload, and implement new cloud-native solutions.

<br />
<Section link="design-principles-s" title="S: Security Prespective" /> 
Help you achieve confidentiality, integrity and availablity (CIA Triangle) of your data and cloud workloads.

<br />
<Section link="design-principles-o" title="O: Operations Prespective" /> 
Helps ensure that your cloud service are delievered at a level that meets the need of your business.

<br />
<Section link="caf-transformation-domains" title="AWS CAF Transformation Domains" /> 
```markdown
- Technology        
    - Using the cloud to Migrate & Modernize legacy infrastructure application data & analytics platforms...
- Process           
    - Digitize, Automation and Optimizing your business operations. 
    - Leveraging new data & analytics to create actionable insights.
    - Using ML to improve customer service experience.
- Organisation: Reimaging your operating model
    - Organise your team around products.
    - Leveraging Agile Methods to rapidly iterate & evolve.
- Product: Reimaging your business model by creating new values propositions (Product & Services) & Revenue Models.
```
