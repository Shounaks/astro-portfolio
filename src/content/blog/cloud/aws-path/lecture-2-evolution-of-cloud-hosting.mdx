<Section link="dedicated-server" title="Stage #1: Dedicated Server"/>
Dedicated Servers are very Expensive to buy and require Capital Expenditure (CapEx) to purchase and setup.
They require high maintainence since electricity bill, real estate is handelled by the company itself.
The whole server runs a single web application.
!["image of dedicated server"](/content/cloud/aws/dedicated_server.svg)

<Section link="virtual-private-server" title="Stage #2: Virtual Private Servers"/>
In this type of servers the single server is still used to a single business entity  but the physical machine is 
virtualized into sub-machines allowing us to run multiple web-applications or websites parallely on a singular hardware.
It has much better utilization of resources as compared to ["Dedicated Servers"](#dedicated-server) but also provides <mark> isolation
of resources</mark> as well.

!["image of Virtual Private Servers"](/content/cloud/aws/vps.svg)

<Section link="shared-hosting" title="Stage #3: Shared Hosting"/>
A single machine, in this paradigm can be used by hundreds of businesses and relies on most tenants under-utilization of their alloted
resources. It is very cheap as compared to the first two stages but has <mark> limited functionality and has poor isolation. </mark>

<Section link="cloud-hosting" title="Stage #4: Cloud Hosting"/>
A <mark>multi-machine</mark> paradigm, that acts as one system. The system is abstracted into multiple cloud services.
It is <mark> Flexible, Secure, Cost-Effective and Highly Configurable </mark>

!["image of Virtual Private Servers"](/content/cloud/aws/cloud_hosting.svg)
