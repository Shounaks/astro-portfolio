<Section link="iaas" title="Infrastructure As a Service" />
<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Provides Building blocks for Cloud IT</li>
    <li>Provides Networking, Computing and Data Storage Space</li>
    <li>Gives us highest level of flexiblity</li>
    <li>Easy to parallel with traditional on-premise IT</li>
    <li>Example: Amazon EC2, GCP, Azure, Rackspace, Digital Ocean, Linode </li>
</ul><br/>
<Section link="paas" title="Platform As a Service" />
<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Remove the need for our organisation to manage the underlaying IT infrastructure.</li>
    <li>Focus on Deployment and Management of Applications.</li>
    <li>Example: Amazon Elastic Beanstalk, Heroku, Google App Engine, Windows Azure</li>
</ul><br/>
<Section link="saas" title="Software As a Service" />
<ul class="list-disc pl-6">
    <li>Complete Service/Product, that is run and managed by the service provider.</li>
    <li>Example: Gmail, Dropbox, Zoom</li>
</ul>
