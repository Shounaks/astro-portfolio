	link="dynamodb-intro"
	title="What is Dynamo DB?"
	imageUrl="/content/cloud/aws/icons/Arch_Amazon-DynamoDB_32.svg"
/>

- Fully managed, highly available alternative to NoSQL Database.
- Replication is done across 3 Availablity Zones (AZs).
- Scales to massive workloads, distributed "<mark>serverless</mark>" databases.
- Millions of requests per second, trillion of rows, and over 100s of TBs of storage.
- Fast and Consistent in peformance!
- Single digit milisecond latency (Extremely Low Latency).
- Integrated with IAM for Security, Authorization and Authentication.
- Low cost & Autoscaling capablities.
- Standard & Infrequent-Access (IA) Table Class.
- DynamoDB is a key/value Database
