<Section link="introduction" title="Introduction" />
Portal that provides customer with on-demand access to  AWS compliance documentation & AWS Terms & Agreements.

<br/>
<span class="text-emerald-400">Artifact Reports</span>
Allows you to download AWS Security & compliance Documents from 3rd party auditors like AWS ISO Certification, Payment Card Industry, System & Organisation Control (SOC) reports.

<br/>
<span class="text-emerald-400">Artifact Agreements</span>
Allows you to view, review, accept & track the status of AWS aggrements such as Business Associate Addendum (BAA) or the health insurance agency (HIPAA), for an Individual account or government.

<br/>
<span class="text-emerald-400">Can be used to support internal audit or compliance.</span>
